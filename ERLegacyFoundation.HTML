About The E.R Legacy Foundation

The E.R Legacy Foundation focuses on providing educational resources for underprivileged kids. We offer annual scholarships to students aspiring to attend college. Our future plans include creating mentorship programs, hosting back-to-school drives, and setting up more community-based resources.
